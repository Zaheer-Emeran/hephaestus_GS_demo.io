Become Part Of The Forge

Volunteering

As a volunteer, there are various activities and events that we plan out for you. Firstly, we would train you on the basics in a specific predetermined field, such as Level Design or 3D Modelling for a two week period. Then, you would travel with us to do our CSR acts, such as Operation: Orphanos where we do activities at Orphanages, or Operation: Young Minds where we go to High Schools and teach them how to create video games.

There are many other benefits to being a volunteer aside from the basic teachings by our professionals, we also provide accommodation/travelling fees to those who live further away, regular healthy meals and a Positive Mental Attitude all around!

If any of these sounds like something that you would like to do, feel free to fill out the form below, or email/whatsapp us for more information. We look forward to working with you!

Sponser

As a Sponser, opposed to a Volunteer, there are many benefits that you would receive. You would work close to our Development Team in terms of what type of Video Game would be produced and would be able to greatly influence which direction the concept and story of the game is portrayed.

You would also be able to, if requested, have your products/services displayed within the game, such as a character using a specific service, or your product being advertised on a billboard for example. You would also have an important hand within creating the actual game itself, be it voicing a character or adding towards the story.

Should you want to become one of our generous sponsers, you can fill out the form out below or contact us!
