Divines Landing

After Ragnorok occured, an event that caused the end of the worlds of Gods and Men, a handful the two beings found sanctuary at a place between time and space, a place where the effects of Ragnorok had not tainted, a place where they could start their new beginnings, a place that they could call 'Home'. This was given the name 'Divine's Landing'

However, Divine's Landing wasn't the only place which escaped the effects of Ragnorok, nor were their residents the only beings left alive. On a place given the nickname 'The Nether', resided the Japanese God of Destruction, 'Yato', with his hoarde of followers.

Yato's only goal? Destroy and Enslave every living being in the universe so that he can rule over all of creation with an Iron Fist. Its up to the Residants of Divine's Landing, along with the God Hephaestus and Goddess Artimes, to protect themselves from Yato's evil reigns.

It's up to you, the product of the two famous Greek Gods, to build up the defences of Divine's Landing and fend of the Rule of Yato before it becomes too late.

On Edge

In the year 2035, the City of Liyue is filled with technology which is far superior than anything the world has ever experienced before, where Mobile Transport such as Cars has become irrelevant. However, you can take mankind out of the Car, but you cannot take the Car out of mankind.

Driving a car, let alone street racing, has become illegal in the City of Liyue, but that won't stop The Underground from doing as they please. The Underground, a well known community of Misfits, Bandits and Outlaws, is where Kennith Brown finds himself one day. After losing it all to the rich at a young age, he is taken in by the Leader of The Underground, Rick Mort, and is taught the ins and out of how to run the economy.

Their goal? Bring down the government by any means necassary. Through hard work and dedication, Kenneth made his way to the top, and after the tragic death which befell Rick, Kennith became the Leader of the Underground.

He finds himself stuck in a conflicting story of Family, Romance and Leadership, Kennith finds it extremely difficult to find a balance between it all. Will he be able to overthrow the Government and keep the people he cares about safe? Or will he cause the City of Liyue to fall?

Thirteenth Sun

"It was peaceful, before it all happened. Our ancestors lived peaceful, normal lives, tending to cattle, growing crops, excluded from the outside world. But suddenly, during the midst of a Blood Moon, the First Sun Appeared, And with it came unthinkable tradgedies."

"Approximately 20% of all living creatures, be it Human, Plant or Animal, began mutating which birthed what we refer to today as unconcious Chimeras. Our Ancestors, those who had survived, had to pool together all their resources, and faced off the Original Chimera and brought peace to their village. However, before the first Sun had time to become legend, a decade later the Second Sun appeared, and with it, even more horrible mutations.

"Although when the Sixth Sun appeared, a small margin of villagers discovered that they could wield the power of the mutations while retaining their conciousness, and thus, the League of Hero's was formed. After each decade, a Sun appeared, and a tournament was held amoungst the Hero's to determine who would fight in the war against the Chimera."

"Things started taking a great turn for the village, until the Village Elder had a vision. 'When the Thirteenth Sun appears on the horizon, choas will befall all of humanity'. As such, four unlikely Hero's were chosen amongst the batch of Hero's, each with their own Skills."

"Each with their own Motive."

Down Below

The realm of Atlantis, a mystical and magical place once filled with prospering residence who had an abundance of gold and jewels, now reduced to nothing but a Lost City under the ocean with its reason to have sunk remaining unknown.

However, when a group of youth stumbled across an artifact called the 'Sea Serpent's Heart' while scooba diving, they decide to have it appraised, only to discover that it was an artifact which would lead them to the legendary Lost City.

After recieving funding and gathering an experienced group of underwater archeologists, the young group began their journey underneath the depths, where no man had ever journeyed, in search for the Lost City. They had spent many, many weeks and thousands of dollars into the exploration of the Lost City, and after long, they were ready.

Little did they know, that while the Sea Serpents' Heart lead them to the Lost City, it also lead the Creatures of the Lost City, to them. Creatures ripened in darkness by the cruel fate known as time, cursed to exist because of the everlasting magic which drowned with the city.

The group of youthful and irresponsible teenagers find themselves unable to return to the surface, unable to call for help, unable to leave the Lost City.

Unable to escape, Down Below.

The Hero's Return

In a world where Technological beings from a distant planet became dominant, humans find themselves on the brink of Extinction. These beings, given the name, 'the Precursors', have deduced that not only were humans unfit to reside on the planet Earth, but that humans in general were obsolete. With no technology inside, with limited knowledge and fragile bodies, the Precursors took it upon themselves to adopt humanity as humans would a stray cat.

Naturally, though, Humans retaliated against the Precursors, angering their King who believed that they were protecting humanity from themselves. And as such, a decree was given amongst the Precursors to slaughter all humans who decided against the rule of the Precursors.

A century later, after the Precursors took over Earth with an brutal rule, a half-breed between the Precursors and Humans emerged when a Precursor felt sorry for an orphaned baby and decided to give the child Precursor knowledge.

The child, a young girl named Holly Mark, grew up to become a woman of drive and determination. She was a natural born laeder, and lived a normal life until her 25th Birthday where the effects of the Precursors' knowledge became apparent.

On the run from deranged Scientists trying to extract her gift as well as strict and lethal Precursors, Holly finds herself struggling to decide whether she wants to become the Bridge between the two worlds, or the catalyst which insights both of their destruction.

Frozen Honour

In a world where Druids, Kings and Magical beasts such as Dragons co-exist, within the Forest called The Grey Zone resides a group of secret beings called 'The Keys' who has the unique and rare ability to summon creatures from a distant realm known as the 'Spirit Realm'. The Keys are able to use the elemental abilities of their familiars which scales based on their level of Mana.

A young boy, Jason Foile, who was nothing more than a exiled peasant villager finds out that not only does he have a powerful Familiar, but it is the Frozen King of all Familiars, 'Tenebrae'. While Jason was still too young to learn how to control the elemental abilities of Tenebrae, he stumbled across the legendary Mage Nicknamed 'Merlin'.

Merlin taught him how to control his powers and raised the boy to use his power for the benefit of Humanity. Little did he know, Tenebrae had different plans.

While Jason wanted to help humanity, Tenebrae wanted to destroy it in order to save all of Magic Beasts before Humans become the reason that they become extinct. Can Jason convince Tenebrae to help him save Humanity, or while Tenebrae convince Jason to get rid of Humanity?
